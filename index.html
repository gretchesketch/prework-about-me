Introduction

For those of you who haven’t already guessed, my name is Gretchen. I am happily married to my best friend which has led me to this exact moment where I am writing this introduction. We have been together for 9 years and married for 6. Once struggling servers no working our way to success. We both knew we wanted more so Chris left his server job and put down his guitar to go back to college. With him in school, working towards two bachelors, I was left to support us financially in the meantime. Waiting tables wasn’t really filling the shoes of two incomes or really three if you take out the music gigs. I kept looking for more lucrative opportunities. I landed at the post office where I have worked for the last 5 and a half years. The hours were even worse than working as a server, but the pay was immensely better and more reliable. This came at a huge cost however, I hardly got to see my husband, I missed family events and holidays, and any hope of a vacation was dashed. It wasn’t until I had moved up seniority and landed the position of regular carrier on one route where things lightened up. Still not ideal. Chris graduated last year and is now a year into his accounting position. This has increased our income significantly allowing me the opportunity to quit my demanding job at the post office and pursue my own education how I see fit. At 29 going back to traditional college just didn’t sound appetizing so I began my search for alternatives where I came across Georgia Tech’s bootcamp programs. The idea of working in IT has always drawn me in so it was kind of a no brainer for me. I applied and am so happy to move on from being a mail carrier and start this new part of my life.
